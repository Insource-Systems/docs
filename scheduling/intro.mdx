### Access the Scheduling App

To access the app, log in to your Insource account. If you do not land on the **My Account** page, click your profile icon at the top right of the page and select **Manage Account**.

<Frame>
  ![Account Menu Header](/images/AccountMenuHeader.png)
</Frame>

You can select apps you have access to on your account home page.
